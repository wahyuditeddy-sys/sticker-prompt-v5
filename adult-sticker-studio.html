How to Use Adult Sticker Studio
🚀 Quick Start (5 steps)
1
Pick a Niche — Choose from 15 trending niches in the sidebar. Click 🔥 Viral for highest demand.
2
Select Sticker Type — Char+Quote (most versatile), Typography (text art), or Char Only (no text).
3
Choose Art Style — Pick one style that matches your niche. Bold Flat = safe & universal.
4
Select Platform — Where will you sell? Each platform has different prompt emphasis.
5
Generate & Copy — Add your quote (or use suggestions), click ⚡ Generate, then copy to your AI tool.
🎯 What Each Tab Does
📄 Prompt — The main AI prompt ready to paste into Midjourney, Gemini, etc.
🔄 Variations — 5 angle variants of your prompt to A/B test designs.
🏷 SEO Tags — Copy-paste tags for Etsy, Redbubble, or Shopee listings.
💡 Tips — Platform-specific advice for selling your sticker niche.

🌐 Platform Guide
🛍 Etsy
Best for US/EU market. PNG transparent background 300dpi. Emphasize uniqueness and handcrafted feel.
PNG 300dpi · Transparent BG · sRGB
🎨 Redbubble
POD (print-on-demand). Uploading white-BG stickers means white edges show — use transparent PNG.
PNG 300dpi · Transparent BG · 1500px+
🏪 Shopee
Southeast Asia market. Cheerful, relatable local content works best. Bahasa Indonesia captions.
PNG/JPG · 1:1 ratio · min 500×500
🖨 Offline/Print
Physical sticker printing. CMYK color mode required. 3mm bleed + safe zone. Prompt auto-adds specs.
CMYK · 300DPI · 3mm bleed · AI/PDF/TIFF
💚 LINE Sticker
LINE store (Japan/SE Asia). Must be transparent background, specific sizes. No adult content.
PNG 370×320px · Transparent · 8 or 16 set
💬 WhatsApp
Personal use / WASticker packs. WebP format, 512×512px. Transparent background essential.
WebP 512×512 · Transparent · <100KB
💡 Pro Selling Tips
💰
Bundle 10–20 related stickers as a "pack" — packs sell 3× better than singles on Etsy.
🏷
Use all 13 Etsy tags. Include long-tail: "funny nurse sticker for water bottle" not just "nurse sticker".
🔄
Use Variations tab to create 5 angles per niche — test which quote + style combo gets more clicks.
📅
Seasonal niches (holidays, back-to-school) should be uploaded 6 weeks in advance for SEO to index.
🎨
Generate in Bold Flat style first — it has the widest appeal and prints cleanly at small sizes.
