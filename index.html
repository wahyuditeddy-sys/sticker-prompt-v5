How to Use Adult Sticker Studio
🚀 Quick Start (5 steps)
1
Pick a Niche — Choose from 15 trending niches in the sidebar. Click 🔥 Viral for highest demand.
2
Select Type — Char+Quote (most versatile), Typography (text art), or Char Only (illustration).
3
Choose Art Style — Bold Flat = safest universal style. Vintage = premium feeling.
4
Select Platform — Choose where you sell. Each platform adjusts the prompt specs automatically.
5
Generate & Copy — Add a quote (optional), click ⚡ Generate. Prompt appears on the right — copy & paste to your AI tool.

💡 Pro Selling Tips
💰
Bundle 10–20 related stickers as a pack — packs sell 3× better than singles on Etsy.
🏷
Use all 13 Etsy tags. Include long-tail: "funny nurse sticker for water bottle" not just "nurse sticker".
🔄
Use Variations tab to create 5 angles per niche — test which combo gets more clicks.
📅
Seasonal niches should be uploaded 6 weeks early for SEO to index before peak season.
